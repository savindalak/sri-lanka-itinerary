Homagama (10–12 April) – 3 Nights

Stay: Local guesthouses in or around Homagama.

Activities: Relax locally; visit the National Museum, Galle Face Green, and local markets.

Monaragala (13–15 April) – 3 Nights

Travel: 190 km (~4 hours)

Stay: Monaragala Rest House or similar.

Activities: Visit Buduruwagala rock carvings and waterfalls.

Colombo (28–30 April) – 3 Nights

Travel: 230 km (~5 hours)

Stay: Cinnamon Grand Colombo

Activities: National Museum, Galle Face Green, local markets.
