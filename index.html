Trip Route Map

Homagama (10–12 April) – 3 Nights

Stay: Local guesthouses in or around Homagama.

Activities: Visit the National Museum, Galle Face Green, and local markets.

Monaragala (13–15 April) – 3 Nights

Travel: 190 km (~4 hours)

Stay: Monaragala Rest House.

Activities: Visit Buduruwagala rock carvings and waterfalls.

Kandy (16–17 April) – 2 Nights

Travel: 150 km (~3.5 hours)

Stay: Cinnamon Citadel Kandy.

Activities: Explore the Temple of the Tooth, Kandy Lake, and the Botanical Gardens.

Habarana (18–19 April) – 2 Nights

Travel: 95 km (~2.5 hours)

Stay: Habarana Village by Cinnamon.

Activities: Visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress & Minneriya National Park.

Anuradhapura (20–21 April) – 2 Nights

Travel: 60 km (~1.5 hours)

Stay: Heritage Hotel Anuradhapura.

Activities: Visit Sri Maha Bodhi, Ruwanwelisaya, and Isurumuniya Temple.

Dambulla (22 April) – 1 Night

Travel: 65 km (~1.5 hours)

Stay: Amaya Lake Dambulla.

Activities: Explore Dambulla Cave Temple & Golden Temple.

Pinnawala (23 April) – 1 Night

Travel: 90 km (~2.5 hours)

Stay: Hotel Elephant Park Pinnawala.

Activities: Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.

Nuwara Eliya (24–25 April) – 2 Nights

Travel: 120 km (~3.5 hours)

Stay: Araliya Red Hotel.

Activities: Visit tea plantations, Gregory Lake, and Victoria Park.

Ella (26 April) – 1 Night

Travel: 55 km (~1.5 hours)

Stay: Awesome Place Ella.

Activities: Visit Nine Arches Bridge & hike Little Adam’s Peak.

Monaragala (27 April) – 1 Night

Travel: 60 km (~1.5 hours)

Stay: Monaragala Rest House.

Activities: Explore Buduruwagala Temple & enjoy the rural landscapes.

Colombo (28–30 April) – 3 Nights

Travel: 230 km (~5 hours)

Stay: Cinnamon Grand Colombo.

Activities: Explore the National Museum & Galle Face Green.
